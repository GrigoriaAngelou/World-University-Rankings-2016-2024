World University Ranking for 2024

The dataset of this project provides a list of universities together with their rankings and various other metrics of interest according to the Times Higher Education.
The Global Rank shows the place that the university is compared to every other university in the world and is computed using different rankings like: 

  Overall Score : Score out of 100 based on teaching, research environment, research quality, industry impact, and international outlook. 
  Teaching : Score out of 100 measuring the university's quality of teaching. 
  Research Environment : Score out of 100 measuring the university's ability to provide a conducive environment for research.          
  Research Quality : Score out of 100 measuring the university's ability to provide a quality research. 
  Industry Impact : The university's effect compare to other university's.
  International Outlook : International outlook is a mark of a top institution, relevant both to international and domestic students.

 You can interact with the dashboard below by selecting the country in which the university of your interest is located and then select the university itself. 

How to read the dashboard:

After choosing the two options on the left of the dashboard you can see:
1. The Global Rank of the university for year 2024.
2. Overal Score for all the years between 2016 to 2024, to observe if this institution is performing better over the years.
3. All the scores for different score types for year 2024 and how they performed compared to previous year.
4. Top 5 universities in the country that your university is located.
5. How many female and male students this university has.
6. How many international students prefer this university.
